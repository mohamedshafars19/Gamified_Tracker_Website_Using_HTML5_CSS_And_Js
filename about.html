Our Mission: Making Rewards Accessible to Everyone

We believe that your everyday activities deserve recognition. Since 2018, we've helped over 500,000 users earn meaningful rewards for things they already do.

Our Story

RewardHub was founded in 2018 by a group of friends who noticed that many loyalty programs were complicated, fragmented, and offered little real value. We set out to create a platform that would simplify the process of earning rewards while providing genuine value to our users.

What started as a small startup with just 3 team members has grown into a thriving community of over 500,000 active users. We've partnered with more than 200 brands to offer a diverse range of rewards, from gift cards to exclusive experiences.

Our journey has been guided by a simple principle: transparency, fairness, and real value. We're committed to creating a rewards ecosystem where everyone wins.

Frequently Asked Questions

How does RewardHub make money?

We earn commissions from our brand partners when users complete offers or make purchases through our platform. This allows us to offer rewards to our users at no cost to them.

Is RewardHub really free to use?

Yes, absolutely! There are no hidden fees or subscription costs. You earn points by completing tasks, and you can redeem those points for rewards at no cost.

How long does it take to receive rewards?

Digital rewards (like gift cards) are typically delivered within 24 hours. Physical rewards may take 7-14 business days for processing and shipping.

Do my points expire?

Points expire after 12 months of inactivity. As long as you're earning or redeeming points at least once a year, your points will remain active.
